Tips, Tricks, and Concepts Learned

Tips and Tricks

  • Create themes to break down content into manageable and enjoyable pieces for users; link them all back to the main page to maintain flow and consistency
    • This also creates a rhythm for users to follow.
  • Use easy to read maps - make sure your maps are as simple, clear, and user-friendly as possible while incorporating cartography that matches your project.
  • Understand the map options before you create a map so that you do not box yourself in due to misunderstanding

Concepts Learned

  • New functionality within Storymaps Beta (such as Guided Tour, Sidecar, etc.)
  • How to properly link app links in a website without errors
  • Proper Time management: This project was very time consuming due to mismanagement of time
