Story Map Process

Using the ESRI Storymap site we were able to create a three part story map (Beginning, Middle and End). By clicking on the plus icon at the bottom of the page, we can then see a list of choices to choose from, Guided tour (beta) is what was chosen.

After inserting the Guided tour (beta) we added 5 slides for the top 5 coordinates of each part of the 2-month journey accordingly. These will need to be updated to include the rest at a later date.

From here we could add in our historic data research of Treaty 9 to our liking.

In order to place the coordinates it was a simple matter of selecting "Edit Location" on the slide.

A new window appeared allowing us to type in the supplied coordinates in the top left of the search bar. Doing this saved a lot of time as we were able to zoom in on our geographic locations and place down the markers.

We could then change the colour of the symbols in the Design tab. The site itself recommends 3 as shown below, or you can pick your own. For this prototype we chose to pick from one of the recommended colours.

Then after that was completed it was duplicated two other time for the middle of the journey and end. A snippet of the final product of the story map is shown below.
