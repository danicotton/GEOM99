Story Map End Product

Click here to view our Story map

Using the ESRI beta StoryMap we were able to create three parts to the story map (Beginning, Middle and End).

After inserting the beta Storymap we added the 5 slides for the top 5 of each part of the 2-month journey accordingly. These will need to be updated to include the rest at a later date.

From here we could add in our historic data of Treaty 9.

In order to place the coordinates it was a simple matter typing in Edit Location and typing in the supplied coordinates at the search bar. Doing this saved a lot of time as we were able to quickly find our location by placing a marker down on it.

We were able to type in our coordinates into the searchbox and hoave it zoom directly to the geographic location.

We could then change the colour of the symbols to those that the site itself recommends or pick your own. For this prototype we chose to pick from one of the recommended colours.

Then after all that is done, we get our final product of the story map.
