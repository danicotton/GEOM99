Where Do We Go From Here?

Onwards to Glory!

In our most recent communication with the client the scope of the project has changed.

Due to the Novel Coronavirus the client will be unable to provide images/photos for the broad public program phase and we shall have to change the storymap focuses appropriately

New Project Scope

  • Producing a hard copy map of the 1905 Treaty no. 9 route: highlighting the Treaty signing communities visited, and combining the coordinate features listed in each of the Crews (rapids, portages, lakes, rivers, etc.) to one map.
  • Creating GIS story maps of the territory in layered themes. These these are as follows:
    • Geographical Features- Lakes and Rivers, Elevation, Ecology, Ecosystems
    • Flora and Fauna- Water Plants, including wild rice, fish, wildlife, trees, birds
    • Impact of Industry along route: Hydro-Electric Dams and Power and Paper Companies
    • Treaty Communities- Arts and Culture, Language, Youth Initiatives; current reserves and former Hudsons Bay Trading Posts

Much of what we have in scope of the storymap can remain; however, due to the lack of images we will have to make it more map focused or aquire stock images

Currently our idea of themed maps is a success with the client; we will meet to discuss with her regarding the added themes we created in our prototype scope
