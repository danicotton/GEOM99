Problems Encountered

Museum Temporary Closure

The Peterborough Canadian Canoe Museum’s temporary closure, due to the Novel Coronavirus has made it difficult to visit or acquire any on-site personally taken pictures that we could use for the project. Due to the layoffs at the Museum our advisor, Shawn Morgan, will be taking over the handling of the project.
